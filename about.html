Hi, I'm Elaine.
who i am

I'm a designer-developer studying CS + Visual Media Studies at Duke University, currently interning at IBM as a Design Intern. I care about design that feels fun! (the kind that makes you forget you're using software).

I come at it from both sides: the systems thinker who wants everything to make sense, and the visual artist who wants it to feel like something.

When I'm not in Figma or Cursor:

  • shooting on film somewhere with good light
  • making playlists for every conceivable occasion
  • drawing in Procreate at an unreasonable hour
  • eating my way through whatever city I'm in
  • curating Pinterest boards I'll never finish
